GoodreadsSync

  • Android
Automatic syncing of reading progress across multiple platforms.

This app is currently in beta testing, in order to download it from Google Play Store please join the Beta Community and become a tester or click this link

An Android application that can automatically sync your ebook and audiobook progress with your Goodreads account. Application is currently under development but an alpha version is available through the play store link at the bottom of the page. Currently only works with Google Books and Smart Audiobook Player with future plans to include Amazon Kindle support.

Technical Overview:
This application works uses several APIs including Goodreads API, Google Books API, and signpost OAUTH API. The application uses RESTful web services and OAUTH1.0/2.0 in order to keep all user's data secure and private.
